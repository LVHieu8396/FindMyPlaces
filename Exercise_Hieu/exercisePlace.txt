Build application named: Find my places
This application can provide user a way to find places around their location. 
It can show route from user’s location to selected places.
Use cases:

- AS a user ,I want to Login with facebook and share my current location
- As a user, I want to see coffee shops around my current location.
- As a user, I want to see restaurant around my current location.
- As a user, I want to see hospital around my current location.
- As a user, I want to see fuel(Petroleum) my current location.
- As a user, I want to see place detail of selected place.
